My Platform

Section #1: Diversity

- Diversify representation in faculty (i.e. Psychological Services, Academic Counselling)

- Stand for more dietary options to include religious & cultural restrictions (i.e. Halal, Kosher)

Section #2: Representation & Inclusivity

- Push for more inclusion in decision-making to involve affiliate colleges and international students

- Spread more awareness about Western's Indigenous Land Acknowledgement

Section #1: Environmental

- Reduce carbon emissions through initiatives like more accessible bike paths

- Increased in-campus transit methods (i.e. underground passages) to reduce use of personal transportation

Section #2: Ethics

- More transparency regarding food sourcing (specifically meat)

- Push for more ethically sourced Purple Merch options

Section #1: Wellness

- Increased dietary options in affiliate college cafeterias and residences (i.e. vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free)

- Introduce wellness credit courses into the curriculum at Western

Section #2: Mental Health

- More accessible mental health services

- Better specialized mental health services for LGBTQ+ and other under-represented communities

Section #1: Continuation of online classes

- A better approach to standardized course delivery

- A more accessible and transparent access to course materials online

Section #2: Transitioning to in-person classes

- Continuation of online resources to allow more flexibility of courses

- Safer access to campus facilities (i.e. gym, pool, labs)
